Flagel Lab News

  • Brianna Ramos, a graduate student in the lab was awarded the Society for Neuroscience (SfN) Trainee Professional Development Award to attend the SfN Annual Meeting in Washington, D.C., in November 2023.

  • Princess Felix, a graduate student in the lab, won a Travel Award to attend the European Behavioural Pharmacology Society (EBPS) meeting in Mannheim, Germany in August, 2023.

  • The Flagel Lab is excited to welcome Stephanie Desrochers, a new postdoc (as of August, 2023) who is coming to us from the Nautiyal Lab at Dartmouth College. Stephanie will be investigating the neural circuitry that modulates cue-motivated behavior and how this interacts with other complex, maladaptive behavioral phenotypes.

  • Amanda Iglesias, a graduate student in the lab, won the Neuroscience Innovator Award at the University of Michigan Neuroscience Conference in June, 2023.

  • Dr. Flagel was interviewed by Dr. Steve Mahler for the #PsychopharmPhriday podcast for her research on sign-tracking and goal-tracking rats and their relevance to addiction. The episode can be found here: "https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=69VpcI1oKXc"a>.

  • Dr. Flagel was interviewed by Gil Eapen for the Scientific Sense podcast for her research on the neural circuitry that contributes to individual differences in vulnerability to mental illness. The episode can be found here: "https://open.spotify.com/episode/1okgUsZdV5twUl2jaH4KOc"a>.

  • Dr. Flagel was interviewed for a recent article in Scientific American that features our work. The article is titled, “Scientists Spot Addiction-Associated Circuit in Rats”, and has been published online.
